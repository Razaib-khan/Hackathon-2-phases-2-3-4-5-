# User Guides

This section provides user-friendly guides for navigating and using the Speckit Plus Todo Application.

## Getting Started

### 1. Account Registration
To begin using the application:

1. Navigate to the application homepage
2. Click on the "Sign Up" link or button (if available)
3. Fill out the registration form with:
   - First name
   - Last name
   - Email address
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)
   - Answer to security question: "Who was your favorite teacher?"
4. Click "Sign Up" to create your account

**Note:** Your security question answer is important for password recovery.

### 2. Logging In
To access your account:

1. Go to the login page
2. Enter your email address and password
3. Click "Sign In"
4. You'll be redirected to your dashboard

## Managing Tasks

### Viewing Your Tasks
1. After logging in, navigate to the "Tasks" section
2. You'll see a list of all your tasks with their current status
3. Use the search and filter options to find specific tasks

### Adding a New Task
1. On the Tasks page, click the "Add New Task" button
2. Fill out the task form:
   - **Title**: Brief description of the task (required)
   - **Description**: Detailed information about the task (optional)
   - **Priority**: Select from Critical, High, Medium, or Low
   - **Due Date**: Set a deadline for the task (optional)
3. Click "Create Task" to save

### Editing a Task
1. Find the task you want to edit in your task list
2. Click the "Edit" button next to the task
3. Modify any fields as needed
4. Click "Update Task" to save changes

### Completing a Task
1. Find the task you've completed in your task list
2. Click the checkbox next to the task title
3. The task status will update to "Completed"

### Deleting a Task
1. Find the task you want to remove
2. Click the "Delete" button next to the task
3. Confirm the deletion when prompted
4. The task will be permanently removed

## Searching and Filtering

### Search Tasks
1. Use the search bar at the top of the task list
2. Type keywords that appear in task titles or descriptions
3. The list will automatically filter to show matching tasks

### Filter Tasks
Filter your tasks by:
- **Priority**: Show only tasks with specific priority levels
- **Status**: Show completed or incomplete tasks
- **Date Range**: Show tasks due within a specific timeframe

## Security Features

### Password Recovery
If you forget your password:

1. Click on "Forgot Password?" on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Provide the answer to your security question ("Who was your favorite teacher?")
4. Follow the instructions to reset your password

### Changing Your Password
1. Navigate to your account settings
2. Click "Change Password"
3. Enter your current password
4. Enter your new password (minimum 8 characters)
5. Confirm the new password
6. Click "Save Changes"

## Account Management

### Updating Profile Information
1. Go to your profile settings
2. Edit your first name, last name, or email address
3. Save changes to update your profile

### Deleting Your Account
**Warning:** Deleting your account will permanently remove all your data.

1. Navigate to account settings
2. Scroll to the "Account Deletion" section
3. Confirm your intention to delete the account
4. Your account and all associated tasks will be removed

## Troubleshooting

### Login Issues
- Ensure your email and password are entered correctly
- Check that Caps Lock is not enabled
- Reset your password if you've forgotten it

### Task Not Saving
- Verify all required fields are filled in
- Check that the title is not empty
- Ensure you're connected to the internet

### Sync Issues
- Refresh the page to reload your tasks
- Check your internet connection
- Log out and log back in to resync data

## Tips and Best Practices

### Organizing Tasks Effectively
- Use descriptive titles for easy identification
- Set appropriate priorities to focus on important tasks
- Add detailed descriptions for complex tasks
- Regularly review and update task statuses

### Maximizing Productivity
- Break large projects into smaller tasks
- Use the priority system to focus on urgent items
- Set realistic deadlines for each task
- Regularly clean up completed tasks
